Building a Symptom-Based Diagnosis Web App

With the rise of digital health tools, we embarked on a mission to simplify early disease detection using AI. Our app allows users to input symptoms and receive instant predictions using a Decision Tree Classifier. This empowers users to take initiative in managing their well-being and understanding potential conditions.

Why Machine Learning in Healthcare?

Machine learning has the ability to recognize patterns in large datasets — perfect for complex symptoms and disease mapping. It provides fast, scalable, and reliable predictions that can support doctors and patients alike in early intervention and diagnosis.

How We Built It

We trained our model using a curated dataset containing hundreds of symptoms and diseases. Using Flask for the backend and Bootstrap for the front-end UI, we ensured a smooth and responsive user experience. We also included features like dietary tips, medication suggestions, and preventive measures for holistic care.

Looking Ahead

We aim to enhance our app with voice input, multilingual support, and integration with wearable health trackers. The future is exciting, and we’re just getting started on this journey to make healthcare smarter, more accessible, and more human-centered.
